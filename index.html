Ramallah

Ramallah is a Palestinian city, and the center of Ramallah and Al-Bireh Governorate. It is located in the West Bank, about 15 km north of Jerusalem, 880 meters above sea level, and has an area of ​​16.5 km². Its population is about 38,998 people, while the population of the governorate is about 328,861 people. Ramallah is adjacent to the city of Al-Bireh until their buildings and streets overlap to appear as one city. Although the city of Al-Bireh is larger in terms of area and population, the most famous of them is the city of Ramallah.
